# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

## General Online Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for every account (include letters, numbers, symbols).  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, messages, or unknown websites.  
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.  
- Do not share personal information (address, phone number, college ID) publicly online.  
- Use only trusted Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi.  

## UPI & Financial Safety Tips
- Never scan a QR code to receive money — scanning always sends money.  
- Do not approve unknown UPI collect requests without verifying the sender.  
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone.  
- Double-check the receiver’s name and UPI ID before making any payment.  

## What to Do in Case of Fraud
- Immediately call Helpline: 1930 to report financial fraud.  
- Report online at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Inform your bank/payment app to block further unauthorized transactions.  

## Final Note
Staying alert and informed is the best defense against cybercrime. Always think twice before clicking, sharing, or paying online.
