#  Responsible Social Media Use Checklist

A simple guide for college students to maintain a safe, respectful, and productive presence on social media platforms.

---

## Do’s

-  **Think before you post**  
  Ensure your content is respectful, appropriate, and does not harm your or others’ reputation.

-  **Protect your privacy**  
  Use privacy settings and avoid sharing sensitive personal information.

-  **Verify information before sharing**  
  Always check the authenticity of news and posts before forwarding or reposting.

-  **Use social media productively**  
  Engage in learning, networking, and skill development opportunities.

-  **Maintain a positive digital presence**  
  Be respectful, kind, and professional in your interactions.

---

##  Don’ts

-  **Don’t share sensitive information**  
  Avoid posting details like your address, passwords, or OTPs.

-  **Don’t engage in cyberbullying**  
  Refrain from trolling, harassment, or posting harmful comments.

-  **Don’t spread misinformation**  
  Do not share unverified or fake news.

-  **Don’t overshare personal life**  
  Excessive sharing can affect your privacy and future opportunities.

-  **Don’t use weak passwords**  
  Avoid simple or repeated passwords across multiple platforms.

---

##  Conclusion

Responsible social media use helps build a strong digital identity, ensures online safety, and promotes a positive and respectful online community.
