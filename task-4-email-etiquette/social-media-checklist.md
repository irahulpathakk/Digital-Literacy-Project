Do’s
-Think before you post — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
-Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information
-Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
-Use social media for learning, networking, and skill development
-Be respectful and maintain a positive digital presence

Don’ts
-Don’t share sensitive personal information (address, passwords, OTPs, etc.)
-Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or harmful comments
-Don’t believe or spread fake news without checking sources
-Don’t overshare private life details that could harm your reputation
-Don’t use weak passwords or reuse the same password across platforms
